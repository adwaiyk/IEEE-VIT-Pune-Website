EcoInnovate Hackathon

Problem Statement

Develop innovative UI/UX solutions to address climate change and environmental challenges.

Hackathon Details

  • Hackathon Duration: 24 hours
  • Participation: Individual
  • Categories:
    • Junior Category (First-Year Students)
    • Senior Category (Second- and Third-Year Students)
  • Registration Period: December 12th, 11:00 PM to December 14th, 11:00 PM
  • Hackathon Dates:
    • Start: December 15th, 6:00 PM
    • End: December 16th, 6:00 PM

Rules & Regulations

1. Disqualification Clause
  • Dual Registration: Participants registered in both categories will be disqualified.
2. General Rules
  • Submission Format:
    • Junior Category: Figma link
    • Senior Category: GitHub link
  • Design Tools: Participants may use tools like Figma, Adobe XD, Sketch, or web development tools.
  • Duration: Submissions must be made within the 24-hour timeframe.
3. Design Guidelines
  • User-Centric Design: Solutions should prioritize user experience and accessibility.
  • Visual Design: Create aesthetically pleasing designs aligned with the theme of climate change.
  • Functionality: Projects should include a working demonstration.
4. Submission Criteria
  • Junior Category:
    • Figma prototype link
    • Project description
    • Optional 3-minute video link showcasing the prototype
  • Senior Category:
    • GitHub repository link
    • Project description
    • Optional 3-minute video link showcasing the website
  • Video Submission Guidelines:
    • Videos must clearly explain the concept and functionality.
    • Ensure the video link is accessible to judges.

Research Sessions

Dive deep into groundbreaking research with sessions led by seasoned professionals and academicians. Enhance your knowledge and skills in an interactive setting.

Our sessions offer a comprehensive introduction to research and design thinking, covering topics such as:

  • Innovation and design thinking project lifecycle
  • Transforming ideas into research papers
  • Conference and journal essentials, including reputable lists and formats
  • Ethical considerations, plagiarism prevention, and AI tools
Participants can connect through a dedicated WhatsApp group for collaboration, project support, and knowledge sharing.
